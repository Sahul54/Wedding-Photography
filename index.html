Congratulations!

I’m so happy you've found the person you want to share your life with. I hope in twenty years, you'll find yourself even happier and more in love than you dreamed possible. I would like to take this opportunity to give you some helpful tips on making your wedding day a “photographic success”.

First, pick a photographer you like, because they will be as close to you on your wedding day as any of your family or friends. They will be there from the moment you start getting ready to the moment the last guest has left.

Second, and most importantly, – HAVE FUN! - Yes, this is one of the most important days of your life, and it is easy to get caught up in the stress of making it perfect. However, go ahead and accept the fact that some things are not going to go as you’ve planned. It will still be a perfect day! Surround yourself with people who are going to be reliable, helpful, and dedicated to you.
