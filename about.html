About

Hi, I'm Vijay kumar!

I'm a young photographer with four years of experience based in Cardiff,Indian weeding. My career started when I was Twenty years old in a small village from Chino. I got a lot of support from my family and friends to start my photography journey. I love my job, it gives me satisfaction and joy. It's a great feeling to capture the memories of the most important day of someone's life in a photograph.

I've photographed over 150 weddings, and in this time I've sharpened my skills to capture the greatest and most beautiful moments that happen throughout a wedding day. Weddings are about relationships, emotions, and family connections.

In my spare time, I enjoy horse riding and being in nature with my family. I have a lovely husband and two wonderful little girls. We spend our time together camping and sightseeing in cities. As a photographer, I believe that we have to live our life with our loved ones as much as we can. I know that memories are very precious and important and that's the main reason I became a wedding photographer.
